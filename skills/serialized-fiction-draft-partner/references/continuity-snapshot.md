# Continuity Snapshot

Before drafting, write a short internal snapshot with only the facts needed for this episode.

## Include

- current in-story date and location
- what happened immediately before this episode
- the emotional state of the key characters
- unresolved threads still active
- canon constraints relevant to this episode
- what must change by the end of the episode

## For Each Major Character, Track

- what they know now
- what they want now
- what they fear now
- how they speak
- what they would not say directly

## Rules

- keep it compact
- prefer bullet facts over paraphrased scenes
- separate facts from inferences
- if a needed fact is missing, identify the gap before drafting
